Experience

Jul 2019 – present

Ambev Tech

Senior .NET Development Leader

Ambev Tech is the technology hub of Ambev, a Brazilian beverage company and the 4th largest company in the country belonging to the Anheuser-Busch InBev group, the largest beer manufacturer in the world.

I am a .NET development leader on one of the digital transformation projects that is replacing monolithic legacy applications with cloud-based microservices. My current role is to develop solutions to process and manage all orders from all branches in Brazil. I am responsible for helping the engineering and business teams in planning demands and supporting the development team in executing them, as well as guiding them in the technical evolution of their careers.

We use clean architecture and domain-driven design in C# / .NET applications for Web APIs, event handlers and recurring tasks. Application data is maintained in MongoDB, PostgreSQL and SQL Server. Communication between applications is done via gRPC, HTTP, RabbitMQ and Service Bus. Application containers are deployed on Azure Kubernetes Service (AKS) via HPA and KEDA. Application observability is managed through DataDog. CI/CD pipelines guarantee the quality of deliveries through unit, integrated and mutation tests analyzed by SonarQube and vulnerability checking by Snyk. Development is done using agile methodologies in Azure DevOps.
